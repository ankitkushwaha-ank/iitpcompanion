About IITP Companion+

IITP Companion+ is an all-in-one platform created to simplify and enhance campus life for students at IIT Patna. From canteen ordering and real-time transport tracking to event alerts and student feedback — we bring everything to one unified platform to make your experience seamless.

Convenience

Order Your Favorite Food with Ease

Choose from a wide variety of meals available at campus canteens and place your order online. Whether you're in a rush or just want to avoid the queue — your meal is a click away.

Real-Time Tracking

Never Miss a Bus Again

With live updates on campus buses and transport, students can plan their movement better, stay punctual, and navigate the campus more confidently.

Alerts & Assignments

Stay Notified

Be informed with timely alerts about classes, club meetings, events, and assignment deadlines. Everything you need is at your fingertips to stay on top of your academic and social life.

Navigation

Explore Campus with Ease

Use our interactive campus map to explore departments, hostels, academic blocks, and other key landmarks around IIT Patna.

Plan Ahead

Event Calendar

Stay updated with upcoming academic events, fests, and club activities using our dynamic event calendar built for students.

Community

Join IITP Clubs

Get involved with various student-run clubs focused on culture, coding, literature, robotics, and much more. A space to explore your passions and talents.

Support

Contact Us

Have questions or suggestions? Reach out to us through our contact page — we're here to support you.

Privacy Policy

At IITP Companion+, we prioritize your data security and privacy. We only collect essential information to personalize your experience and maintain a safe, reliable platform for all users.

Secure Login

Your login credentials and session data are encrypted using industry-standard protocols.

Minimal Data

We collect only the information required to deliver core services and improve usability.

No Third-Party Sharing

Your personal data is never sold or shared with advertisers or third-party services.

Cookie Control

Cookies are used strictly for functional and performance optimization — not for tracking.

Feedback Anonymity

All student feedback is anonymized and securely processed for service improvement.

Data Removal

You may request data deletion or account removal at any time via our contact form.

Assignment Privacy

Academic assignment data remains private and is never visible to anyone except you.

Location Privacy

Transport tracking uses anonymous data and does not retain your personal movement history.
